Andrographis paniculata

(Fah Talai Jone)

Drug/dose for adults

  • Take Andrographis paniculata capsules or tablets that contain Andrographis paniculata extract or crushed powder (crude drug), which indicates the amount of substance andrographolide in mg per capsule or as % of the dose.
  • Calculate the substance andrographolide 180 mg/person/day. Divide into 3 doses before meals for 5 consecutive days (if the number of capsules is large, divide into 4 doses per day).
  • Start the drug as soon as possible after SARS-CoV-2 (COVID-19) infection.

Dose in children

  • There is not enough information to recommend its use in children for the treatment of COVID-19 Patients should consult a medical professional.

Precautions/common side effects

    Contraindications: Do not use in the following conditions:

    • People with a history of allergic reactions to Andrographis paniculata.
    • Pregnant women/may be pregnant and women who are breastfeeding. Theoretical data suggest that uterine contraction and fetal abnormalities may be affected.

    Precautions:

    • Abdominal pain, diarrhea, nausea, palpitations, loss of appetite, dizziness (more common with high or prolonged use).
    • Hives or anaphylaxis (in rare cases) may occur.
    • Should not be used with other antiviral medications.

    Side effects:

    • Concomitant use with antihypertensive and anticoagulant drugs such as warfarin, aspirin and clopidogrel since their effects may double.
    • No available information on dosage adjustments in patients with severe kidney disease or liver disease.
    • Adverse effects may be caused by other drugs being used together.
