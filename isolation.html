Guide to Isolation Period for COVID-19 Patients

  1. COVID-19 patients who have no symptoms (asymptomatic) should isolate themselves in their homes or places allocated by the government for 10 days from the day of the diagnosis (for provinces encountering difficulties in the available of hospital cots, patients stay in the hospital for 5-7 days and continue their isolation until the 10th day at home).
  2. COVID-19 patients with mild symptoms should isolate for 10 days from the first day that the symptom(s) is/are present. In case fever persists after the 10 days, they should continue to isolate until the symptoms and fever lessen for at least 24-48 hours.
